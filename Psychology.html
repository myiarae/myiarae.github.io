Psychology at the University of Maine

Why Psychology?

Ever since the first Psychology course I took during my sophomore year of high school, I have been fascinated with how the human mind works in all aspects. Abnormal psychology has just recently sparked my interest as well!

Abnormal psychology studies abnormal behaviors which interfere with an individuals daily functioning. I am particularly interested in the diagnostic processes and treatment plans that go along with disorders such as schizophrenia, obsessive compulsive disorder, or even generalized anxiety disorder.

I chose to attend the University of Maine in Orono, not only because it is only two hours from my hometown, or because of the in-state tuition, but because of the environment that came with the university.

My older brother, Adam, attended the University of Maine, Orono, so growing up I would always see him over vacations or we would talk on the phone and would tell me all of these fun and exciting stories about living here.
